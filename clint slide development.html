Our Story: Passion on a Plate

Since 2017, Bays Peri Peri has been serving the heart of Reading with more than just food; we serve an experience. Our journey began with a simple mission: to share our passion for authentic, flame-grilled peri peri, using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients. We believe in bold flavours, fresh cooking, and creating a welcoming space for our community.

From our diverse menu featuring unique tastes like our Chicken Tikka burgers to our commitment to providing delicious Halal options, every dish is a reflection of our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.
